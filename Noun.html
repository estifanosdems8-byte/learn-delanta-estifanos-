What is a Noun?

A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.

Types of Nouns

  • Common Nouns: dog, city, book
  • Proper Nouns: John, London, Bible
  • Abstract Nouns: love, freedom, happiness
  • Collective Nouns: team, family, flock
